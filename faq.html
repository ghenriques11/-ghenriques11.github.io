Questions & Answers

1. How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

Printing a document from your laptop at HZ is straightforward. Follow these steps:

  1. Visit hz.mynetpay.nl and select "Mobility Print".
  2. Download and install the Mobility Print app for your operating system.
  3. Choose the file(s) you want to print and select "HZ Printer".
  4. Locate a printer on campus and either scan your student card or enter your HZ login credentials.
  5. Tap "Print Release", select your document, adjust the settings as needed, and hit print!

2. How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?

To scan a document and send it to your laptop, follow these instructions:

  1. Log in to a printer using your HZ student card or login details.
  2. Select the "Scan" option and place your document(s) on the feeder or glass plate.
  3. Adjust the settings as preferred and press the start button.
  4. Don’t forget to log out from the printer when you’re finished!

3. How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event) on the HZ web shop?

To make a purchase on the HZ web shop, follow these steps:

  1. Go to webshop.hz.nl and use the navigation bar to choose between "HZ", "HZ Cult", and "HZ Sport".
  2. Browse the available products, and select the items you wish to purchase.
  3. Once you've added everything to your cart, go to the shopping cart, select "Checkout", and complete your payment.

4. How can you book a project space?

To book a project space at HZ, follow these instructions:

  1. Log in to MyHZ and navigate to the self-service portal in the personal menu.
  2. Select "Reservations", then "Reserve a meeting or project room", and finally "Reserve a room".
  3. Choose the date from the top left, and select the time from the top row.
  4. Click and drag to choose your desired time slot in the preferred room, then click "Next". Fill in your details and confirm your reservation.

5. What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

If you need to park at the HZ parking lot, here’s what you should do:

  1. If parking at the Groene Woud Campus is not an option, use the parking lot on Kousteensedijk.
  2. This lot has designated spaces for visitors, staff, and students of Het Groene Woud.
  3. The parking lot is an 8-minute walk from the HZ building.
  4. Present your entry card at the JRCZ service desk, located next to the HZ building, to receive an exit card.
