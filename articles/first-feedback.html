First Feedback

Introduction

In this section, I will share my thoughts on the feedback I received from the SKC (Student Knowledge Check) assignment. This feedback was crucial in guiding my learning and development throughout the course.

Feedback Received

The feedback from the SKC assignment highlighted several key areas for improvement:

  • Top and Side Menus: It was noted that I need to implement a top menu and a side menu for better navigation.
  • FAQ Responses: I need to answer the frequently asked questions to provide more comprehensive information.
  • Content Paragraphing: The content was too dense, and it was suggested to break it into more readable paragraphs.

Actions Taken

Based on the feedback, I have made the following updates:

  • Top and Side Menus: Implemented both top and side navigation menus for improved user experience and accessibility.
  • FAQ Responses: Added answers to the frequently asked questions to address common queries and provide clearer information.
  • Content Paragraphing: Reformatted the content into shorter, more digestible paragraphs for better readability and engagement.
